BrainStation

Visit brainstation.io

the challenge —

To create a more cohesive, multi-product experience for an evolving userbase; while launching new products that push the boundaries of traditional education.

overview —

As BrainStation continues to grow and evolve as an industry leader in career changing education, so to does the online experience. During my time at Konrad Group, alongside two other designers, we worked to help further solidify the overall BrainStation brand, both visually and from a user experience perspective.

This execusion was implemented on both core products: the public facing marketing site and the student facing Synapse Learning Portal.



Custom city illustrations were created to identify BrainStation's multiple global campuses.

checkout flow —

The checkout flow is one of the most important parts of the BrainStation product. Not only does it allows students to commit to their decision of further education, but it also helps them find a campus, course time, and payment plan catered to them.

The previous BrainStation flow was a two step process; one screen indicating all the details in a list format and the second a confirmation of their payment. Through user research this was found to be quite intimidating to many users, as the cost break down / summary view was an information overload.

The updated flow changed this, allowing a user to walk through a step by step experience creating this catered style of course creation as they went along. While they still have a summary view before final payment, the initial screens help ensure their on the right track for success.

student facing portal —

Alongside the marketing site, the Synapse Learning Portal gives students access to a full suite of education tools; these include project briefs, weekly lessons, online lectures, attendence, project submission, and more.

The platform encourages student engagement, overall course participation and communication; both student to student and student to educator.

This main site rebrand was also applied to newly relaunched technology blog.
