Houston?

I’m Brock, a designer, space enthusiast, and an advocate for using design as a gateway to improve the lives of anyone and everyone.

I have always been drawn to understanding how people and products interact. Whether that’s myself struggling for the third time that day to pull a push door, or watching my friends decide if a cold brew at 4pm is the right decision, each makes me rethink the potential for better solutions.

I live and work in Toronto, where I have been designing digital experiences at Konrad Group since my graduation from York / Sheridan’s Bachelor of Design program. I’ve also recently re-entered the world of code and am working on some exciting side projects with my friends through a design collective called Smolhax.
