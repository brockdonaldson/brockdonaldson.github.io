I’m a designer, space enthusiast, and am constantly trying to make my apartment look like it's from the 70's.

I live and work in Toronto Canada, where I'm currently designing to create a smarter home at ecobee. Before that, I was designing digital experiences at both Konrad and BrainStation. And before that, I was studying at the York / Sheridan’s Bachelor of Design program.

I have always been drawn to understanding how people and products interact. Whether it's pulling a push door for the third time that day, or rationalizing a cold brew at 4pm with friends, the smallest moments make me rethink opportunities for better solutions.

I’m always interested in trying new things or collaborating on projects. If you’d like to get ahold of me, shoot over an email to brockmdonaldson@gmail.com.
