Vita Donor App

[CHALLENGE]

To create an application that helps streamline the blood donation process, all while explaining important donation information.

The app allows donor to learn about mutliple stages of the donation process.

This blood donation information app is meant to act as a way for donors to understand the donation process during the wait period in a donation clinic. iPad stations could be transported along to each donor site, allowing individuals easy access to the relevant information.

The app includes information about pre- and post-donation procedures, interactive blood typing info-graph, common deferral information, as well as future donation booking on the final screen.

[PRINT VERSION]

Physical information pamphlets would also be offered to those interested in a take-home alternative. These pamphlets cover the same information while at the same time offering a unique user experience from the iPad experience.

[LEARNING OUTCOME]

I was able to gain a greater understanding on presenting complex topics in a way that it could be understood by a general audience.
