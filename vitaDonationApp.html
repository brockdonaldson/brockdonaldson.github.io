Vita Donor App

CHALLENGE

To create an application that helps streamline the blood donation process, while also articulating key donation information to users.

Donors are able to learn about each stage of the donation process through detailed infographics.

This app is meant to act as a bridge between donors and the blood donation process. Meant to be displayed on iPads, these stations could be transported along to each donor site, allowing individuals easy access to the relevant information.

The app includes information about pre- and post-donation procedures, interactive blood typing info-graph, and common deferral information. Once the process is complete, users are able to book their next appointment.

PRINT VERSION

Physical information pamphlets would also be offered to those interested in a take-home alternative. These pamphlets cover the same information, while at the same time offering a unique user experience.

LEARNING OUTCOME

I was able to gain a greater understanding on how to present complex topics to a broader audience.
